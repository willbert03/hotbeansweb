About Us

At Hot Beans Web, we’re brewing up fresh, innovative web solutions that help businesses thrive online. Founded in 2016, our small but passionate team has been serving up bespoke websites, e-commerce platforms, and custom web applications for clients of all sizes — from local start-ups to household names like Kara’s Cakes, New Look, and Amazon. Every project is carefully crafted with a blend of creativity and technical expertise, delivering websites that not only look great but perform smoothly. We pride ourselves on attention to detail, roasting every line of code to perfection and never cutting corners. Whether you’re looking to refresh your brand or need a full-scale digital platform, our team will spill the beans on the best solutions to help your business grow. At Hot Beans Web, we believe great websites — like great coffee — should always be fresh, bold, and built to energise.
